GUProgress.js

A nanoscopic progress bar. Featuring realistic trickle animations to convince your users that something is happening!

GUProgress.start() — shows the progress bar

GUProgress.set(0.4) — sets a percentage

GUProgress.inc() — increments by a little

GUProgress.done() — completes the progress
